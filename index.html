About Us

This site was made to be a hub of volunteer opportunities for students at Marlboro High School! However, this doesn't mean it's limited to just Marlboro High School students. Anyone can use this site to find listings, but it is predominantly geared towards Marlboro High Schoolers!

Why Use This?

The point of this site is to simply be a directory for volunteer opportunity posts that're looking for volunteers, as well as giving students a chance to be able to find opportunities that fit their needs and wants for volunteer hours and/or work experience.

How The Site Works

This site runs primarily on user submissions. Users can submit volunteer opportunities they find to be posted on the site for others to see via the submission on our Contact page. These submissions are then reviewed by the site owners before being posted to ensure quality and legitimacy of the opportunities. Once approved, the opportunities will appear on the Service page for users to browse through and find ones that interest them.
